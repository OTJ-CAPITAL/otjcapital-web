The opportunity

Africa's financial markets are structurally mispriced. Retail participation in crypto and forex is growing faster than anywhere in the world, yet institutional-grade quantitative infrastructure barely exists.

OTJ Capital is building that infrastructure — and capturing the alpha that comes with it. Our AI engine scans markets every hour, generates signals from multiple quantitative strategies, sizes positions by volatility-adjusted risk models, and executes orders automatically.

No manual trades. No emotional decisions. No human bottlenecks.

We are not building a trading tool. We are building a fund.

EMA Momentum

Trend Following

Buys when multiple momentum signals align simultaneously. No single indicator is sufficient — all must confirm.

02

Bollinger Mean Reversion

Statistical Reversion

Captures statistically extreme price dislocations. Requires price to be more than 1.5 standard deviations below mean.

EntryPrice ≤ lower Bollinger Band
FilterRSI below 35 — oversold
ConfirmZ-score ≤ −1.5
ExitPrice ≥ upper band, RSI > 65

Why Trust Us

Built on discipline,
not promises.

Every principle below is enforced in code. Not policy documents. Not guidelines. Hardcoded rules the engine cannot override.

🔒

Capital is Protected First

The risk engine runs before every single trade. No position opens without approval. If the kill switch triggers — at 15% drawdown or 5% daily loss — all trading halts immediately and automatically.

📊

Full Investor Transparency

Every trade, every signal, every position is logged and visible to investors in real time via the OTJ App dashboard. No monthly PDFs. No selective reporting. You see exactly what the engine sees.

🤖

Zero Human Emotion

No trader overrides. No gut decisions. No panic selling. The AI executes the strategy exactly as designed — every hour, every day, in bull markets and bear markets alike.

⚖️

Strict Position Limits

Maximum 1% of AUM at risk per trade. Maximum 5% per position. Every position sized by ATR — volatility determines size, not conviction. The math protects you, not a manager's confidence.

📐

Minimum 2.5× Reward Ratio

No trade is taken unless the expected gain is at least 2.5× the risk. At a 29% win rate the fund breaks even. This asymmetry is why systematic funds survive long-term when discretionary ones don't.

🌍

Built for African Markets

Founded in Nairobi, designed for African capital. We understand the volatility, the liquidity constraints, and the structural inefficiencies of this market better than any foreign fund ever could.
